3.2 Includes - HTML Footer

Define the footer html, such as the closing body tag and js. This should be accompanied by a call to {{> kss-includes.html-header.hbs}} to open the tags.

    <script src="/theme/libraries/jquery-1.9.1.min.js"></script>

</html>
